This is the story page for TPoP. Here will be a general summary/outline.

To go more in-depth than the description on Smackjeeves and The Nuzlocke Forums, TPoP is a story following many young Pok√©mon who must learn to work around their disabilities. Their journey involves them discovering that they are capable of more than they were ever led to believe, or perhaps realizing that they will never break through their limitations. As such, they must find other ways to reach their goal. Or perhaps you'd say they.. "Follow their own Pouvoir"?

Since the last thing I want to do is misrepresent any disorders/disabilities that I use in the comic, I do close research on each and every one and apply first-hand and second-hand experience when writing my characters' behaviors, personality, etc.
