TPoP

Steven is a recurring character in TPoP. Pouvoir met him when sent on a mission to deliver a letter to him in Dewford. He is the leader of Team Geo and— unbeknownst to Pouvoir— the region's Champion.
As of November 2019, his only appearance is in Granite Cave.
