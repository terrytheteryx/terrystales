This is the story page for ClV. Here will be a general summary/outline.

C'est la Vie is a simple Y nuzlocke following Xavier, a boy who has moved to Kalos from Kanto to become an intern for Professor Sycamore. He befriends two other interns, Yvonne and Zoey, and sets off on his journey with them.
The events of this story do not have any effect on TPoP.
