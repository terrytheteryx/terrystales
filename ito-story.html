ItO

Story is what develops the characters!
This is the story page for ItO. Here will be a general summary/outline.

To put it simply, ItO is essentially a retelling of Pokémon Ranger: Guardian Signs, except formatted to fit the PMD-verse. Rather than humans, Pokémon are the only ones to be seen here.
The story follows a teenaged Blitzle named Cardinal who has been separated from her partner, Hunter the Skarmory. The Ranger Guild sent them to the Oblivia region to resolve a sudden abundance of Mystery Dungeons, before something terrible went wrong as they arrived via Magnagate.
The events of this story occur about 6 years before TPoP.
