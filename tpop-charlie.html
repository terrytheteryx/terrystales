Charlie is one of two main characters in TPoP. She is a Grovyle and one leader of Team Pouvoir.

Appearance

Compared to other Grovyle, Charlie looks recognizable yet clearly different. She has stout legs and a rectangular body. In addition, her eyes have large arches above them, similarly to a Heliolisk. On less noticeable topics, she has three tail leaves compared to two, her wrists do not have leaves, and she has three toes and fingers compared to two. Her crest leaf is also comparably shorter and drifts on her left side.
